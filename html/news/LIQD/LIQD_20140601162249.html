Hall of Fame Member

Douglas J. Von Allmen

Class of 1965

Year Inducted: 2001

 

 

Douglas J. Von Allmen grew up in Louisville, except for two years his family lived in Miami, Florida. He graduated from Atherton High School upon returning to Louisville after his father's death. After high school graduation, Doug worked in a blue-collar job at a cardboard box factory. Douglas came to the University of Kentucky in 1962 to study accounting and earned his way working as a counselor in the dormitories, helping keep books for small businesses and working in the University's credit union. A member of Delta Tau Delta social fraternity, he graduated with highest honors with a BS in accounting in 1965.

Upon graduation, Doug became a public accountant with KPMG Peat Marwick in St. Louis, Missouri. Doug has made leveraged acquisitions of businesses for himself and his partners since 1977. In recent years, he sold some of these holdings, including a chain of 350 sunglass stores that was merged with the Sunglass Hut International. He focused his time on Beauty Alliance, Inc., a company that is comprised of fourteen professional beauty supply distributors.

Doug and his wife Linda reside in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida. Doug and Linda have four children, Dean Kretschmor, Laura Kretschmor, Julie Skurer, and David Von Allmen. Doug and Linda gave the University a very generous gift in recognition of this gift, the School of Accountancy and the Center for Entrepreneurship have been named in their honor.
