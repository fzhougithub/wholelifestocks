    Nanosphere (NSPH) Slumps: NSPH Plunges 14.3% in Session

    By Zacks Equity Research September 5, 2014 7:05 AM
    • 
    • 
    • 
    • 
    Content preferences

      Nanosphere, Inc. (NSPH) saw a big move last session, as the companys shares fell more than 14% on the day. The move came on pretty good volume too with far more shares changing hands than in a normal session. This continues the recent downtrend for NSPH, as the stock is now down 54.2% in the past one-month time frame.

      This slump shouldnt be too much of a surprise to investors, as the company has seen 4 negative revisions in the past few weeks and its current year consensus for loss estimates has moved higher over the last 30 days. This suggests there may be more trouble down the road. So make sure to keep an eye on this stock going forward to see if this recent slump will continue, as the earnings picture definitely suggests that this might be the case.

      NSPH currently has a Zacks Rank #4 (Sell) while its Earnings ESP is zero.

      Some better-ranked stocks in the healthcare sector include Gilead Sciences Inc. (GILD), Cambrex Corporation. (CBM) and China Biologic Products, Inc. (CBPO). All these stocks carry a Zacks Rank #1 (Strong Buy).
