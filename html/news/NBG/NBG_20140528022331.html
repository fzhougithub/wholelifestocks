National Bank of Greece (ADR) (NYSE:NBG): Fiscal Results For Q1 To Be Released

Posted In Financial - By Grant Murphy On Tuesday, May 20th, 2014 With 0 Comments

Dallas, Texas 05/20/2014 (ustradevoice) – National Bank of Greece (ADR) (NYSE:NBG) has made an announcement through a recent press release dated May 13, 2014 that the company would be releasing the first quarter financial results for the FY2014. The bank has also made an announcement regarding the fact that the company’s more than 1,136 million shares would begin trading at the Athens stock exchange at the rate of EUR 2.20 per share of the subscription price.

Announcement regarding the results for Q1

The National Bank of Greece has made an announcement regarding the fact that it would be releasing the financial results for the Q1 of FY2014 on May 28, 2014. National Bank of Greece (ADR) (NYSE:NBG) would also be organizing a conference call in order to facilitate a proper discussion of the results with the media related persons and other parties that might be interested such as the analysts and investors, on the same day.

The shares to begin trading

National Bank of Greece reported that these 1.136 billion shares would have voting rights. After the addition of these shares, the new total of the company’s shares would amount to more than 3.53 billion shares of the common stock with voting rights and a nominal value of EUR 0.30 per share.

The Stock Markets Steering Committee of Hellenic Exchanges approved the trading of the new shares on the Athens Stock Exchange on May 16, 2014. These 1.136 billion shares would be credited to the investor securities accounts of the beneficiaries in DSS on May 20, 2014 at the commencement of the trading of these shares.

The official website of the National Bank of Greece (ADR) (NYSE:NBG) contains all the necessary details for the trading of these shares under the section called Investor Relations. The company reports in the press release that the Shareholder Services Department of the National Bank of Greece must be contacted for further details.
