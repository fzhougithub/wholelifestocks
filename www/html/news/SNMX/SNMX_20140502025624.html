Senomyx, Inc. discovers, develops, and commercializes flavor ingredients for the packaged food, beverage, and ingredient supply industries using proprietary taste receptor-based assays and screening technologies. The company's programs focus on the development and commercialization of sweet, savory, and salt flavor ingredients; bitter blockers; and cooling agents. It licenses flavor ingredients to its collaborators on an exclusive or co-exclusive basis. The company has product discovery and development collaborations with Ajinomoto Co., Inc., Firmenich SA, Nestl SA, and PepsiCo, Inc. Senomyx, Inc. was founded in 1998 and is headquartered in San Diego, California.
