E-Commerce China Dangdang Faces An Uphill Climb In China's Highly Competitive E-Commerce Market

Mar. 31, 2014 11:28 PM ET  |  About: DANG by: James Sands This article is now exclusive for PRO subscribers.

When E-Commerce China Dangdang (DANG) went public in late 2010, the company was touted as being the Amazon.com, Inc. (AMZN) of China. At this time, Alibaba Group and Jingdong Technology Group Corporation or JD.com, Inc., were not high on the radar screen, and Vipshop Holdings Limited (VIPS) had only been operating for a couple of years.

Today, Dangdang now has four years of growth and operations under its belt. However, the company is losing market share to Alibaba, JD.com, and Vipshop.
