Dendreon Cleared to Continue Operations as It Seeks a Sale

Daily Bankruptcy Review ,
Peg Brickley,
November 12, 2014,
(c) 2014 Dow Jones & Company, Inc.

Dendreon Corp . on Wednesday cleared its first bankruptcy court hearing, launching a process in which lenders owed $620 million have agreed to take less than half that amount from anyone willing to buy the cancer-drug-making business.

Judge Peter Walsh of the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Wilmington, Del., signed orders allowing the Seattle biotechnology company to continue providing Provenge, the prostate cancer treatment that failed to catch on with doctors, while it heads toward a sale or reorganization....
